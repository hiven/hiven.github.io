Information
  • What should I wear? We hope our wedding will offer you a good excuse to dress up! Mostly we want our guests to be comfortable so we do not expect you in your formal black tie, think late summer garden party. Our ceremony will be outdoors (Great British weather permitting...) so please bear in mind there are lots of grassy areas, as well as stone chippings - so perhaps best to avoid stilettos!
  • What facilities are on-site? If you are staying in the tents, there are permanent shower and toilet blocks for you to use. The rooms in the house and barns are all en suite (some have a bath too!)
  • What do I need to bring with me? The usual bits, plus a swim costume, Hawaiian shirt and something to drink on Friday. For those in tents, a bed will be setup with bedding and pillows. However, you will need to bring a towel.
  • Can I park my car on site? Yes! Plenty of parking is available.
